Mission

Promoting the Use of Innovation and Emerging Technologies for the Greater Good

To take advantage of the great breakthroughs in emerging technologies for the health and wellbeing of humanity, as well as the other beings with whom we share the planet; and to do so in such a manner that promotes social justice and equality.
To inspire, educate and provide our fellow human beings with the “toolbox” (contextual knowledge, critical thinking habits, and analytical skills) for applying ethical values to scientific and technological innovation.
To define ethical standards by which to measure actions taken within the scientific community, as well as in the development, deployment, and application of emerging technologies.
To create a world in which ethical values go hand-in-hand with scientific and technological innovation.
To focus our attention on ethical leadership, the ability to exercise moral judgment, creativity, and courage in the practice of leadership.
To think continually about how emerging technologies can best be used to promote social justice and economic equality.
To develop technologies which enhance affordability, accessibility, inclusiveness, and equity within educational institutions as well as workplaces.
To develop new leaders who are committed to ethical standards for scientific research and development.
To develop cooperation and teamwork within the scientific and technological research groups.
